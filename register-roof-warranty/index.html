Roof Warranty Registration FAQ

Common questions about submitting your warranty registration with Planet Roofing.

What information should I include in the warranty notes?

Include any available project details such as completion date, invoice number, permit number, roof material, manufacturer, or any notes listed in your contract or warranty paperwork.

Can I register more than one roof type?

Yes. Select every roof system or service that applies to your project, such as tile roofing plus waterproofing, flat roofing plus TPO, or roof repair.

Does this form replace manufacturer warranty registration?

No. Some manufacturers may require separate registration or documentation. This form helps Planet Roofing keep your local project record organized.

Can warranty coverage be affected by other contractors working on the roof?

Yes. Warranty coverage can be affected if other contractors alter, damage, penetrate, or modify the roof system. Contact Planet Roofing before any additional roof-related work when possible.
