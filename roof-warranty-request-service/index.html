How Warranty Service Requests Are Reviewed

A roof leak can come from the roofing system, HVAC equipment, plumbing vents, wall openings, skylights, stucco, windows, gutters, or work performed by other trades. The inspection helps identify the source.

1. Submit Request

Send your contact information, address, roof type, and a clear description of the issue.

2. Review & Scheduling

Planet Roofing reviews your request and contacts you for additional details or an inspection appointment.

3. Leak Inspection

A leak inspection may be performed to determine whether the issue is related to roofing work completed by Planet Roofing.

What Can Cause a Roof Leak?

Not every leak is caused by the roof membrane, tiles, shingles, or metal panels. A professional inspection helps separate roofing-related issues from other building or mechanical causes.

Roofing System

Membrane seams, flashings, underlayment, tiles, shingles, edge metal, and penetrations are reviewed.

Other Trades

HVAC, plumbing, satellite, solar, stucco, windows, and other work on or near the roof may affect watertightness.

Maintenance Items

Clogged drains, blocked gutters, standing water, debris, and unsealed penetrations can create leak symptoms.
