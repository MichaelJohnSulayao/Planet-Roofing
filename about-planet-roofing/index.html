Meet the Owner

Gentian Kraja’s Journey Into Roofing

Back in the early 2000s, Gentian Kraja found himself in New York working for a friend’s roofing company. It was not the career he originally envisioned, especially after earning a degree in Business Administration, but it became the start of a long roofing journey.

After moving to Florida, he saw how complex local building regulations, insurance claims, permits, and roof approvals could be for homeowners. That experience shaped Planet Roofing’s mission: help property owners navigate roofing challenges with honesty, patience, and care.

Today, Planet Roofing is a family-owned roofing company in Florida with a growing team focused on empathy, integrity, improvement, and earning customers’ trust every day.
