Tile Roofing Built for Florida Curb Appeal and Long-Term Value

Tile roofing is one of the most recognizable roof systems in South Florida. It works well on Mediterranean, coastal, modern, and luxury homes because it gives the property a high-end look while offering strong protection when installed correctly.

Planet Roofing installs and replaces tile roofs using approved roofing assemblies, proper underlayment, flashing details, ventilation considerations, and code-compliant fastening methods. Our team can help you compare Westlake tile roofs, Boral tile roof colors, concrete tile, clay tile, and other roof product options before you make a final decision.

For many homeowners, the hardest part is not choosing tile roofing — it is choosing the right color and profile. A roof color can change the whole appearance of the home, affect HOA approval, and influence how the house looks with stucco, stone, pavers, gutters, fascia, and exterior paint.

Tile Roof Profiles and Styles

Tile profile matters just as much as color. Some homes look best with a bold barrel tile, while others look cleaner with flat tile or slate-style concrete tile.

Spanish S-Tile Flat Tile Double Roman Mission / Cap & Pan Slate Look Shake Look
  • S-curve tile: gives a traditional Spanish and Mediterranean roof appearance.
  • Flat tile: works well for cleaner, more modern South Florida homes.
  • Barrel tile: creates a luxury tile roof look with strong shadows and depth.
  • Slate-look tile: offers a refined appearance with concrete tile durability.
  • Shake-look tile: can deliver a wood-style look without natural cedar maintenance concerns.

Westlake and Boral Tile Roof FAQs

Are Westlake and Boral tile roofs good for Florida homes?

Yes. Concrete and clay tile roofs are common in Florida because they offer strong curb appeal, long-term value, and good performance when installed according to Florida Building Code and approved roof system requirements.

Which tile roof color is best for a Florida home?

The best tile roof color depends on the home exterior, HOA rules, neighborhood style, and personal preference. Terracotta, espresso blends, brown gray, charcoal, and blended barrel tile colors are all common choices in South Florida.

Can Planet Roofing help with HOA approval?

Yes. We can help provide tile color information, product details, and roofing documents commonly needed for HOA and permit review.
