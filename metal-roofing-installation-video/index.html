Professional Standing Seam Metal Roof Installation

This project video shows the installation progress of a standing seam Galvalume metal roof by Planet Roofing in Fort Lauderdale, Florida.

Standing seam metal roofing is a premium roof system with vertical metal panels and concealed fasteners. Instead of exposed screws on the main roof field, the panels are secured with clips and covered seams, creating a clean look and helping reduce common leak points.

For South Florida homeowners, a properly installed metal roof can help improve durability, energy efficiency, wind performance, and long-term roof value. Planet Roofing focuses on code-compliant installation, clean detailing, and clear communication from inspection to final cleanup.

What This Metal Roof Installation Includes

Every roof is different, but a professional metal roofing project usually includes careful preparation, waterproofing, panel layout, trim work, and final inspection.

Roof Deck Review

Before the new metal system is installed, the roof deck should be inspected for damaged wood, soft areas, or issues that may need repair.

Waterproof Underlayment

High-performance underlayment helps protect the building from wind-driven rain and creates a secondary water barrier below the metal panels.

Panel Layout & Clips

Standing seam panels are measured, aligned, and fastened with a concealed clip system that supports clean lines and thermal movement.

Flashing Details

Wall transitions, valleys, penetrations, edges, and roof-to-wall areas receive careful flashing details for long-term leak protection.

Code-Compliant Work

Planet Roofing installs roofing systems with attention to Florida Building Code requirements and local permitting procedures.

Final Cleanup

After installation, the job area is cleaned and the roof is reviewed so the finished system looks professional and performs properly.
