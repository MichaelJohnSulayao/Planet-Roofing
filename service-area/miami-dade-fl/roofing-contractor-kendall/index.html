Kendall Roofing FAQ

Do I need a permit for roof work in Kendall?

Yes. Most roof replacements and many repairs require permits. Planet Roofing helps with permit documentation, NOAs/product approvals and inspection coordination.

How much does roof replacement cost in Kendall?

Pricing depends on roof size, slope, material, decking condition and code requirements. Shingle roofs are usually lower cost than tile or metal, while flat/TPO systems vary by assembly and insulation needs.

Is tile or shingle better for Kendall homes?

Both can work well when installed correctly. Shingle roofing is often more affordable, while concrete tile and metal roofing can offer a longer-lasting upgrade depending on budget, HOA rules and structure.

Can you help after a storm?

Yes. We can inspect storm damage, provide temporary protection when needed, document roof conditions and quote repair or replacement options.
