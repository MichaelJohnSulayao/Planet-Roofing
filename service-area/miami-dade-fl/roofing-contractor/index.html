Common Roofing Problems

Roof Problems Across Miami-Dade County

Miami-Dade properties often experience roof leaks from cracked tile, missing shingles, aging underlayment, flat roof ponding, failing flashings, clogged drains, old roof vents and storm-related damage.

Coastal areas may also experience more salt-air exposure, while inland properties can still face strong heat, humidity, wind-driven rain and hurricane-season roof stress.

Best Time to Call a Roofer

  • After heavy rain or wind damage
  • Before hurricane season
  • When stains appear on ceilings or walls
  • When shingles, tiles or metal panels are damaged
  • Before buying, selling or insuring a property
  • When your roof is near the end of its expected service life
