Wind uplift: South Florida wind events test roof edges, ridge areas, fasteners and roof-to-wall details.

Older roofs: many Miami Gardens homes have roof systems that may be near the end of their life, especially if repairs have been delayed.

Miami Gardens Roofing FAQs

Do you provide roof repair in Miami Gardens?

Yes. Planet Roofing provides roof repair in Miami Gardens for leaks, storm damage, shingles, tile, flashing, roof vents, pipe boots, valleys and flat roof issues.

Should I repair or replace my roof?

If the damage is small and the roof is still in good condition, a repair may be enough. If the roof is older, leaking in several places, has brittle materials or underlayment failure, replacement may be more cost-effective long term.

Do you work on tile, metal and flat roofs?

Yes. We work on shingle, tile, metal, flat, TPO and modified bitumen roof systems for residential and commercial properties.
