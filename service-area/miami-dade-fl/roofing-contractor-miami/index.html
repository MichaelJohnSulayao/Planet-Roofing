Miami Roofing FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a permit for roof work in Miami?

Most roof replacements and many repairs require permits. Planet Roofing can help prepare documents, product approvals and inspection coordination for Miami roofing projects.

How much does a roof replacement cost in Miami?

Cost depends on roof size, slope, material, decking condition, access, tear-off requirements and permit needs. A written estimate is the best way to price the project correctly.

Should I repair or replace my Miami roof?

If the roof is newer and the damage is isolated, repair may be enough. If the roof is older, leaking in multiple areas, has widespread wear or is causing insurance issues, replacement may be the better long-term option.

What roof material is best for Miami?

Shingles, tile, metal, TPO and modified bitumen can all work depending on the property. The best choice depends on roof design, exposure, budget, neighborhood requirements and warranty goals.

Do you offer financing for Miami roof projects?

Yes. Financing options are available for qualified homeowners who want to move forward with roof repair or replacement while managing monthly payments.
