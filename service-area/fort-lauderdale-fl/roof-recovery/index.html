Roof Restoration

Need to Revive Your Old Roof?

Roof recovery, also called roof restoration, is a budget-friendly way to improve your roof’s look and performance. This process uses coatings, treatments, repairs, and protective layers to help correct minor leaks, worn surfaces, fading, and weather-related wear.

Florida’s weather is tough on roofing systems because of hot sun, heavy rain, humidity, and hurricane exposure. A properly planned roof recovery can add a new layer of protection, reduce water intrusion risk, and help your roof handle South Florida conditions better.

FAQ

Roof Recovery Questions

What is roof recovery?

Roof recovery is the process of restoring or improving an existing roof with repairs, coatings, treatments, or a new protective layer when the roof is still suitable for recovery.

Is roof recovery better than roof replacement?

It depends on the condition of the roof. If the roof deck and main system are still strong, recovery can be a smart and cost-effective option. If the roof has major damage or trapped moisture, replacement may be better.

What types of roofs can be recovered?

Many flat roofs, commercial roofs, and some residential systems may qualify. The best option depends on material, age, moisture condition, drainage, and code requirements.

How do I know if my roof qualifies?

A professional inspection is the best way to know. Planet Roofing can inspect your roof, review leak areas, and recommend recovery, repair, or replacement.
