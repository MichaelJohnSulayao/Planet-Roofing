Local Roof Problems

Common Roof Issues in Seven Isles

Salt-Air Corrosion

Fasteners, vents, flashing, gutters, drip edge and metal accessories can corrode faster near canals and the Intracoastal.

Storm Wind Uplift

Wind pressure tests roof edges, shingles, tile fastening, metal panels, underlayment and low-slope roof membranes.

Flat Roof Ponding

Modern waterfront homes often include low-slope sections that need proper slope, drains, scuppers and membrane details.

Tile Underlayment Aging

Tile may look fine from the ground while underlayment, valleys and penetrations are the real leak source.

Flashing Leaks

Roof-wall intersections, chimneys, skylights, vents and parapet transitions are common leak areas.

Hurricane Season Damage

Wind-driven rain and debris can expose small weaknesses that become major roof leaks during storms.
