Common Roof Problems in Idlewyld

Moisture Exposure Continuous humidity and tidal moisture can affect underlayment, decking, flashing, and roof accessories.
Salt Air Conditions Salt air can impact fasteners, metal details, edge metal, and roof penetrations over time.
Wind-Driven Rain Storms can push water into weak flashing, valleys, roof edges, and roof-to-wall transitions.
Tile & Flat Roof Issues Many waterfront homes use tile or low-slope sections that need proper repair, drainage, and waterproofing.

Roof Repair or Roof Replacement?

Some roof leaks can be repaired when damage is limited. If the roof is old, leaking repeatedly, has underlayment failure, damaged decking, storm damage, or insurance concerns, roof replacement may be the better long-term investment.
