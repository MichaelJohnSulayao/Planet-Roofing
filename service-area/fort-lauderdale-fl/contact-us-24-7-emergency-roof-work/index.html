Emergency Checklist

What to Do Before We Arrive

  • Move furniture, electronics and valuables away from the leak.
  • Place buckets or towels under active water entry.
  • Take photos and short videos of leaks and visible damage.
  • Do not climb on a wet, steep or storm-damaged roof.
  • Send photos by text to help us understand the roof condition faster.

FAQ

Emergency Roofing FAQs

What should I do during a roof leak emergency?

Protect the interior first by moving valuables, placing buckets under active leaks and avoiding unsafe roof access. Then call a licensed roofing contractor for inspection and temporary protection.

Does Planet Roofing provide emergency roof tarp service?

Yes. Planet Roofing can help with emergency roof protection, temporary tarping and urgent roof repair requests when conditions allow safe access.

What information should I provide for emergency roof work?

Provide your name, phone number, property address, city, zip code, roof type, building type, description of the issue, photos if available and whether safe roof access is available.

Do emergency roof repairs need permits?

Some temporary emergency protection may be performed quickly, but permanent roof repairs and replacements may require permits depending on the scope and local jurisdiction.
