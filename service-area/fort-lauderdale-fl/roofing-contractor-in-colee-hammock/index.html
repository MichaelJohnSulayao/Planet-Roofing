Common Roof Problems in Colee Hammock

Cracked or Slipped Tile Clay and concrete tile roofs can crack, shift, or expose underlayment during storms and aging.
Old Roof Decking Older homes may have damaged or weakened decking that should be checked during roof work.
Flashing Leaks Roof-to-wall details, valleys, vents, and penetrations are common leak areas.
Tree and Debris Damage Mature trees can drop branches, leaves, and debris that clog drains and damage roofing materials.

Roof Repair or Roof Replacement?

Not every roof problem requires a full replacement. Some leaks, broken tiles, flashing issues, or small damaged areas can be repaired. However, if the roof is old, leaking repeatedly, has widespread underlayment failure, or has major storm damage, replacement may be the better long-term choice.
