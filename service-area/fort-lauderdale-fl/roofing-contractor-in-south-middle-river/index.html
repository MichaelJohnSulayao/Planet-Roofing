Local Roof Problems

Common Roof Issues in South Middle River

Humidity and Moisture

Constant moisture can shorten underlayment, sealant and flashing life if the roof is not detailed correctly.

Storm Wind Damage

Wind-driven rain can expose weaknesses around roof edges, shingles, tile fastening and roof penetrations.

Flat Roof Ponding

Low-slope sections need proper drainage, scuppers, drains and membrane details to prevent leaks.

Shingle Wear

Older shingle roofs can show granule loss, lifted tabs, brittle edges and leak-prone flashing.

Tile Underlayment Aging

Tile can look good while the underlayment, valleys and penetrations are failing below.

Flashing Leaks

Roof-wall intersections, vents, skylights, chimneys and parapets are common leak sources.
