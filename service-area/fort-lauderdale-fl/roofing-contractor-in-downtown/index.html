Common Roof Problems Downtown

Flat Roof Drainage Commercial and low-slope roofs need proper drains, scuppers, slope, and waterproofing to reduce ponding water.
Roof Penetrations Vents, AC units, equipment stands, skylights, pipes, and wall connections are common leak points.
Storm Damage Wind-driven rain can expose weak areas, loosen flashing, and damage roof edges.
Aging Roof Systems Older roofs may have worn underlayment, damaged decking, open seams, or repeated leak history.

Roof Repair or Roof Replacement?

Some roof leaks can be repaired if the damage is isolated. If your roof is old, leaking repeatedly, has widespread membrane or underlayment failure, has storm damage, or is causing insurance concerns, roof replacement may be the better long-term investment.
