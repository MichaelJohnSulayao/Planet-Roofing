Roof Repair or Roof Replacement?

Some roof leaks can be repaired when damage is isolated. If the roof is old, leaking repeatedly, has underlayment failure, widespread membrane damage, poor drainage, storm damage, or insurance concerns, roof replacement may be the better long-term investment.
