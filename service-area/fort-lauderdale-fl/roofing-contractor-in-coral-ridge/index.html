Common Roof Problems in Coral Ridge

Waterfront Exposure Salt air, wind, and humidity can affect roof fasteners, metal details, flashing, and coatings over time.
Tile Roof Wear Tile roofs can develop cracked tiles, loose pieces, underlayment failure, and valley leaks.
Flat Roof Drainage Low-slope sections need proper drainage, scuppers, flashing, and waterproofing to reduce ponding water.
Storm Damage Heavy rain and wind can lift materials, damage roof edges, loosen flashing, and expose weak areas.

Roof Repair or Roof Replacement?

Some roof problems can be repaired, especially when damage is isolated. However, if the roof is old, leaking repeatedly, has underlayment failure, widespread damage, or insurance concerns, roof replacement may be the smarter long-term option.
