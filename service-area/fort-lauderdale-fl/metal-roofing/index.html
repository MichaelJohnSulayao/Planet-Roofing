Metal roofing is one of the strongest, longest-lasting roofing options for South Florida homes and commercial properties.

Metal Roofing FAQs

Common questions about metal roofing in Fort Lauderdale and South Florida.

How long does a metal roof last?

Metal roofs are highly durable and can last 40 to 70 years depending on the metal type, installation quality, coating, roof maintenance, and coastal exposure.

Are metal roofs energy efficient?

Yes. Metal roofs reflect solar radiant heat and can help reduce cooling costs. Reflective coatings and proper ventilation can improve energy performance.

How do metal roofs perform in extreme weather?

Metal roofs perform very well in heavy rain, high winds, and harsh weather when installed correctly with approved panels, underlayment, flashings, and fastening systems.

Will a metal roof increase my home’s value?

A metal roof can increase buyer confidence because of its durability, long life, low maintenance, and premium appearance.

What maintenance is required for a metal roof?

Metal roofs need regular inspections for loose fasteners, damaged panels, clogged gutters, sealant wear, flashing issues, and debris buildup.
