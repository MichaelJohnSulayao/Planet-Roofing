Aging Shingle Roofs Older shingles can curl, crack, lift, lose granules, and become more vulnerable to leaks.
Vintage Ventilation Issues Poor attic ventilation can trap heat and moisture, reducing roof life and comfort.
Tile Roof Repairs Older tile roofs may have cracked tiles, slipped tiles, valley leaks, and worn underlayment.

Roof Repair or Roof Replacement?

If damage is isolated, roof repair may be enough. If the roof is old, leaking repeatedly, has widespread shingle wear, damaged underlayment, soft decking, ventilation problems, or insurance concerns, roof replacement may be the smarter long-term investment.
