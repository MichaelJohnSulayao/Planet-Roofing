Common Roof Problems in Lauderdale Lakes

Leaks Around Vents

Old boots, pipes, skylights and roof penetrations can crack or separate during heat and heavy rain.

Flat Roof Ponding

Low-slope roofs can hold water if drains, scuppers or slope are not working properly.

Missing or Lifted Shingles

Wind and age can loosen shingles, exposing underlayment and decking to moisture.

Damaged Tile

Broken tiles can allow water to reach the underlayment, especially on older tile roofs.

Soft Decking

Older buildings may have plywood or decking damage hidden under existing roof layers.

Poor Past Repairs

Temporary patches can fail quickly if the actual leak source is not located and corrected.
