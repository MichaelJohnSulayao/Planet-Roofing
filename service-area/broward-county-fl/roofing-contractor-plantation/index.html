Plantation Roofing and Solar FAQs

Do Plantation roof projects require permits?

Yes. Roof replacements and many larger roof repairs typically require permits and inspections. Planet Roofing helps prepare project information, roofing product approvals, and inspection coordination for qualifying work.

Can you work on a roof with solar panels?

Yes. If solar panels affect the roofing area, we can coordinate the roof scope around solar panel removal, reinstall timing, and waterproofing details. Solar work should be handled by properly qualified solar professionals when required.

What roof is best for Plantation homes?

The best roof depends on your home, HOA rules, budget, slope, existing structure, and long-term goals. Popular options include architectural shingles, concrete tile, standing seam metal, flat roof systems, and energy-efficient materials.

Do you help with HOA roof color and material approvals?

We can help provide roof material information, product details, and color options that homeowners can submit to their HOA or association when approval is required.

How fast can you inspect a roof leak in Plantation?

Same-day scheduling may be available depending on the day, weather, and crew availability. For active leaks, call or text 954-600-1462 with photos if possible.
