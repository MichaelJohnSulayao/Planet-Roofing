Why Beachfront Roofs Need a Different Approach

Salt Air Corrosion

Ocean air can corrode exposed metals, low-quality fasteners, vents, flashing, and roof accessories faster than inland properties.

Wind Uplift

Edges, corners, underlayment, and roof attachments must be installed with Florida wind requirements in mind, especially close to the Atlantic.

UV and Heat

South Florida heat breaks down weak materials, sealants, and coatings. Durable products and correct installation help extend roof life.

Flat Roof Drainage

Many beachside buildings use flat or low-slope roofing. Proper slope, drains, seams, and flashing details help reduce leaks and ponding issues.

Condo and HOA Needs

Lauderdale-by-the-Sea has many condos and association-managed properties. We can help coordinate scopes, material options, and project planning.

Storm Readiness

Roof inspections before and after major weather events can help find weak points before small issues become expensive interior damage.
