FAQ

Weston Roofing Questions

How much does roofing cost in Weston?

Pricing depends on roof size, roof material, slope, underlayment, tile selection, decking condition, permits, and HOA requirements. Larger Weston tile roofs usually cost more than simple shingle roofs because of material cost and roof complexity.

Do I need a permit for roofing work in Weston?

Yes. Roof replacement and many significant repairs require proper building permits and inspections. Many Weston communities also require HOA or architectural review approval before the roof work begins.

Can you repair a tile roof leak without replacing the whole roof?

In many cases, yes. If the issue is limited to a valley, flashing, broken tile, vent, or smaller underlayment area, a roof repair may be possible. A roof inspection is needed to confirm the correct repair.

What roof types does Planet Roofing install in Weston?

Planet Roofing works with tile roofing, shingle roofing, metal roofing, flat roof systems, TPO roofing, roof coatings, and roof waterproofing solutions depending on the property and project requirements.
