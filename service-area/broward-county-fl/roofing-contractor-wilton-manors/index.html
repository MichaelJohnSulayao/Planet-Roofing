Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a roof cost in Wilton Manors?

Pricing depends on roof size, pitch, deck condition, material type, permit requirements and hidden damage. A smaller shingle roof may cost much less than a large tile, metal or flat roofing system.


Do I need a permit?

Yes, roof replacements and many larger repairs typically require a building permit. Planet Roofing can help with the permit process and required product approvals.


Can you repair instead of replace?

Yes. If the roof still has useful life, we can recommend repairs. If the roof has widespread damage, repeated leaks or an expired system, replacement may be safer.
