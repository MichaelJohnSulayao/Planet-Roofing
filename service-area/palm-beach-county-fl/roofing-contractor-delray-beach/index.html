Storm & Coastal Roof Problems

Common Roof Issues in Delray Beach

Wind Uplift

Storms test roof edges, fasteners, underlayment and flashing. Proper installation details help reduce wind vulnerability.

Salt Air Exposure

Homes near the coast can experience faster corrosion of flashing, fasteners, vents, gutters and roof accessories.

Tile Roof Damage

Concrete and clay tile roofs need proper walking, fastening, underlayment and flashing details to avoid leaks and cracked tiles.

Flat Roof Ponding

Low-slope sections need proper membrane, slope, drains and flashing details to reduce standing water and leaks.

Heavy Rain Leaks

Wind-driven rain can enter weak roof-wall transitions, vents, valleys and penetrations when details are not sealed correctly.

Ventilation Problems

Heat and moisture buildup can shorten roof life, affect decking and create comfort issues inside the home.

Delray Beach Roofing FAQs

What does a roof replacement cost in Delray Beach?

Roof replacement cost in Delray Beach depends on roof size, roof type, slope, material selection, access, deck condition, permit requirements and whether the home needs tile, metal, shingle, flat roofing or specialty waterproofing details.

Are permits required for roofing in Delray Beach?

Yes. Roof replacements and many significant repairs require permits through the City of Delray Beach or the applicable Palm Beach County jurisdiction. Work must meet Florida Building Code and local inspection requirements.

What roofing materials work best for Delray Beach homes?

Common Delray Beach roofing options include concrete tile, clay tile, standing seam metal, architectural shingles, TPO and modified bitumen for flat or low-slope sections. The best choice depends on budget, HOA rules, roof design, coastal exposure and long-term maintenance goals.

Does Planet Roofing repair storm damage in Delray Beach?

Yes. Planet Roofing can inspect and repair storm-related roof damage including leaks, lifted shingles, broken tiles, flashing issues, fascia damage, flat roof membrane damage and temporary roof protection needs.
