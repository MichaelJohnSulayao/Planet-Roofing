Common Roof Issues in West Palm Beach

Hurricane Wind Uplift

Storms test roof edges, fasteners, underlayment and flashing. Proper installation details help reduce wind vulnerability.

Heavy Rain Intrusion

Seasonal downpours can expose weak valleys, wall transitions, roof penetrations and flat roof drainage issues.

UV and Heat Damage

Florida sun can age shingles, coatings, sealants and membranes. Climate-rated materials improve roof performance.

Flat Roof Ponding

Low-slope commercial and residential sections need proper slope, drains, membrane and flashing details.

Tile Roof Wear

Concrete and clay tile systems require strong underlayment, flashing and fastening details for long-term protection.

Commercial Roof Leaks

Retail, office and multifamily buildings often need targeted roof inspections, waterproofing and drainage corrections.

FAQ

West Palm Beach Roofing FAQs

How much does roof replacement cost in West Palm Beach?

Roof replacement cost in West Palm Beach depends on roof size, material selection, roof pitch, deck condition, waterproofing needs, building height, access and permit requirements.

Are permits required for roofing in West Palm Beach?

Yes. Roof replacements and many significant repairs require permits through the City of West Palm Beach Building Division or the applicable Palm Beach County jurisdiction. Work must meet Florida Building Code and inspection requirements.

What roofing systems are recommended in West Palm Beach?

Common West Palm Beach roofing options include high-wind architectural shingles, concrete tile, clay tile, metal roofing, TPO, modified bitumen and reinforced waterproof flat roof systems. The right system depends on the roof design, exposure level and maintenance goals.

Does Planet Roofing repair storm damage in West Palm Beach?

Yes. Planet Roofing can inspect and repair storm-related roof damage including leaks, lifted shingles, broken tiles, roof edge damage, flashing problems, flat roof membrane damage and temporary roof protection needs.
